üß† How It Works

The system automatically generates meaningful names by:

  • Extracting key words from bill titles (safety, education, health, etc.)
  • Removing common words like "relating to", "an act", etc.
  • Adding appropriate suffixes like "Act", "Bill", or "Program"
  • Keeping it concise - maximum 40 characters
